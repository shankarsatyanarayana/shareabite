Our Services

Food Listing Service

Function hall managers can list their leftover food items on our platform. By doing so, they can reduce food waste and provide an opportunity for individuals to collect and distribute surplus food to those in need.

Food Pickup and Distribution Service

Individuals can volunteer to collect and distribute surplus food from function halls to communities and individuals in need. Our platform facilitates the coordination and logistics of food pickup and delivery, making it easy for volunteers to make a positive impact.

Educational Resources

We provide educational resources and information about food waste reduction, food safety guidelines, and ways individuals can get involved in fighting hunger in their communities. Our goal is to raise awareness and empower individuals to take action against food waste and food insecurity.
